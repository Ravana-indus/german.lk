Au Pair in Germany from Sri Lanka — A Safe, Structured Pathway

Au Pair is a cultural exchange route that can support your language and life experience in Germany. German.lk helps you confirm eligibility, prepare documentation, and move forward with a contract-first, portal-based workflow.

Pathways Au Pair

Who This Is For

  • Candidates seeking cultural exchange + German exposure
  • Applicants who want guided documentation and a clear plan
  • Those who want process clarity and transparency

Minimum Requirements

Education

A pass in A Levels is required.

Language

German A1 Level or higher is required.

Age Criteria

Applicants must generally be between 18 to 26 years old.

Finances

No Blocked Account is typically required, as your host family provides room, board, and pocket money.
