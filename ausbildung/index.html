Ausbildung in Germany from Sri Lanka — Learn, Earn, Build a Future

Ausbildung is a practical pathway where you train and work in Germany. German.lk helps you choose the right route, prepare a strong file, and execute with a standardised checklist + portal tracking.

Pathways Ausbildung

Who This Is For

  • Students and young adults who want a practical career path
  • Those who prefer structured training over academic routes
  • Applicants ready to follow timelines and document standards

Minimum Requirements

Education

A pass in A Levels is required.

Language

German B1 Level or higher is required.

Finances (Blocked Account)

A Blocked Account may be required if your monthly training stipend (Ausbildungsvergütung) does not cover the minimum living cost threshold set by the German government.
